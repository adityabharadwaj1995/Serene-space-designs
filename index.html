FAQ

Before you begin, here are answers to what clients ask most.

Ingrid Hase
Lead Designer at Haus®
Ask a question →
How long does an interior project take?
+

Most residential projects run between 3 and 12 months depending on scope and build complexity. A single-room concept might wrap in 6 weeks. A full renovation with custom joinery and sourcing from abroad takes longer — and we'd rather be honest about that than over-promise.

Do I need an architect already, or do you handle that?
+

We work with your existing architect or can recommend trusted partners we've collaborated with for years. On smaller projects with no structural changes, we often handle all technical drawings ourselves. We'll advise you on the right setup at the start.

Can I be involved, or do you handle everything?
+

As much or as little as you'd like. Some clients love the process — they join site visits, weigh in on every material choice. Others prefer to hand off fully and arrive to a completed space. We tailor our process to how you work best.

Do you work outside your home country?
+

Yes. We've delivered projects in Italy, the Netherlands, Portugal, and the UK. International projects involve additional planning, but we have established networks of contractors and suppliers in several European markets. Reach out and we'll tell you honestly whether it's feasible.

How do payments work?
+

We work on a 30% deposit, milestone-based structure for larger projects, or a simple 50/50 split for consultations and concept packages. We keep it transparent and fair, and everything is outlined before you commit.
